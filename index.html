Things To Do In Waterford County

Waterford is one of the oldest counties in Ireland, and is renowned for its fantastic amenities and landscape.
From surfing to shopping, there's something to do for everyone!
Being located in the heart of the "Sunny South-East" - it's no wonder waterford is such a huge tourist attraction.

Filled with historic sites and lush green river valleys,a stunning coastline and a vibrant city, this website will help you to plan your visit, from how to get here to what things to do during your visit.
