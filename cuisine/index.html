Cuisine

With an emphasis on local produce served in relaxed settings, Waterford City has something to offer to foodies everywhere. Whether you are looking for casual dining during the day or a superb night time dining experience, we have everything that you need within a short walking distance. Local restaurants offer fantastic value with many offering 'early bird' or special menus throughout the week.

Cafés

Café culture is becoming increasingly popular in Ireland and Waterford city is no exception. The city is dotted with lovely cafes serving fresh foods, pastries and beverages. For those who love to linger over lunch, Waterford city cafés are the perfect places to while away the hours or meet with friends.
Check out : Carter's Chocolate Café, George's Court Shopping Centre, 8 Barronstrand Street, Waterford , 051 841802

Casual Dining

Waterford has many restaurants that offer a casual dining experience. Come along for a freshly cooked meal served with a side of relaxation!

Chinese Cuisine

A wide variety of cuisines are available in Waterford, however one of the most popular is Chinese cuisine. With Chinese restaurants and takeaways spotted throughout the county, you're sure to find one you love.

The Blaa

Don't leave Waterford City without trying out the world famous 'Blaa', a floury bread product exclusive to Waterford City eaten mainly at breakfast time.
Its introduction to Waterford City has been credited to the French Huguenots who settled in Waterford City in 1693. They brought pain blanc to Waterford which was corrupted by Waterfordians to blaa.
Locals enjoy their blaa with a 'rasher' of bacon or some 'Red Lead' (luncheon sausage) in the middle as a mid morning snack. Other popular fillers include cheese and onion crisps and sliced ham, which the locals pronounce "hang".
